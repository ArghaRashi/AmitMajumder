My Story

I am Amit Majumder, from Kolkata. My journey started as a simple travel vlogger, but my path took a unique turn. My deep connection with Bangladesh, further solidified by my marriage to a Bangladeshi woman, placed me in a special position. I became more than a YouTuber; I became a bridge for people on both sides of the border.

My audience, largely Bengali-speaking people from both India and Bangladesh, started reaching out to me not just for travel tips, but for guidance on complex issues like visas and cross-border marriages. I realized there was a genuine need for a trusted guide who understands the nuances of both cultures and bureaucracies.

Today, my work extends to geopolitical analysis and providing concrete solutions to the very real challenges people face. This website is an extension of my mission: to connect our people, share our stories, and offer professional help where it's needed most.
