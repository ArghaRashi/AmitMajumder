My Story: A Bridge Between Two Bengals

I am Amit Majumder, from Kolkata. My journey started as a travel vlogger, but my path took a unique turn. My deep connection with Bangladesh, further solidified by my marriage to a Bangladeshi woman, placed me in a special position. I became more than a YouTuber; I became a bridge for people on both sides of the border.

My work now extends to geopolitical analysis and providing concrete solutions to the very real challenges people face. This website is an extension of my mission: to connect our people, share our stories, and offer professional help where it's needed most.
